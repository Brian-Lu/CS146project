Rock

Musically, the sound of rock is traditionally centered on the amplified electric guitar which is typically supported by an electric bass guitar and percussion produced from a drum kit that combines drums and cymbals. This trio of instruments has often been complemented by the inclusion of other instruments, particularly keyboards such as the piano, Hammond organ and synthesizers.

History

The foundations of rock music are in rock and roll, which originated in the United States during the late 1940s and early 1950s, and quickly spread to much of the rest of the world. You can read more about the history of Rock here.
